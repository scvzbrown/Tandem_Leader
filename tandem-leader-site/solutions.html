Inquire About This Solution

Not every church is in a season to hire a full-time Executive Pastor. But every thriving church needs that level of senior operational leadership.

The Executive Pastor on Retainer offering gives your church access to experienced, senior-level XP expertise on a fractional basis. Think of it as having a trusted, seasoned Executive Pastor in your corner — available to support your senior pastor, coach your staff, and help shepherd your organization's operations — without the full-time cost.

What's Included

  • Ongoing availability for senior pastor support and strategic counsel
  • Staff coaching and leadership development
  • Operational health and systems oversight
  • Strategic planning guidance and execution accountability
  • A trusted outside voice for sensitive organizational decisions

Why Fractional?

Many churches — especially those in the 200 to 800 attendance range — are at a stage where they need XP-level leadership but aren't ready (or resourced) for a full-time hire. A fractional Executive Pastor gives you the expertise, the perspective, and the relational investment of a senior leader, at a fraction of the cost.

Best For Churches in transition (search for new staff, leadership change, rapid growth), growing churches adding staff for the first time, or senior pastors who need a trusted operational partner so they can focus on what they're called to do.
